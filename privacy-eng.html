Privacy Policy

Scope: ShowDirect Inc. ("Company", "we") — mobile game JangSaNyang and related services

Effective date: 2026-01-01

ShowDirect Inc. respects users' privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we collect and use it, retention periods, third-party disclosures, and users' rights.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate and improve our services. The following types of information may be collected.

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, etc.
  • App usage data and access logs
  • Advertising identifiers: IDFA, ADID (where permitted)

1.2 Information Collected When Necessary

  • Purchase history: transaction identifiers (we do not collect payment credentials such as credit card numbers)
  • Email address: provided when contacting customer support

We do not, as a rule, collect directly identifying personal information such as name, address, phone number, or government-issued identification numbers.

2. How We Collect Information

Information is collected through the following methods:

  • Automatic collection during gameplay
  • User-submitted data when contacting customer support
  • App store platforms (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store, One Store) for payment processing

3. Purpose of Information Use

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating game services
  • Improving gameplay experience and service quality
  • Debugging and error analysis
  • Responding to user inquiries
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Delivering advertisements and aggregated analytics (non-identifiable)

4. Data Retention Period

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, and we securely delete data when the retention period expires.

Data Type Retention Period Legal Basis
Access logs 3 months Communications privacy regulations
Purchase records (transaction IDs) 5 years E-commerce regulations
Customer support records (emails, etc.) 3 years Consumer protection laws

When retention periods expire, electronic files are permanently deleted using irreversible methods, and physical records (if any) are destroyed by shredding or incineration.

5. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Information may be disclosed only in the following cases:

  • When the user has given explicit consent
  • When required by applicable laws or legal processes

6. Data Processing by Third Parties

To operate our services, we may use third-party service providers listed below; these providers process data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

  • Google LLC — Cloud infrastructure, analytics
  • Firebase — Backend services, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — Advertisement delivery

7. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data
  • Request restriction of data processing

Requests can be submitted via the contact information below; we will respond without undue delay.

8. Data Destruction Procedures

When personal data is no longer required, electronic files are permanently deleted using irreversible methods, and physical records (if any) are destroyed by shredding or incineration.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Restricted access control
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is identified, it will be deleted immediately.
